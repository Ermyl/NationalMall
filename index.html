Destination Retail & Entertainment — Built BIG to be the Attraction.

Nine regional hubs across Aotearoa New Zealand. 150–200k m² each. 350–400 stores. Clean energy on the roof, public transport at the door, and a full day out inside.

See the idea Investor overview

The idea

A national network of destination-scale shopping and entertainment centres that combine retail, leisure, clean energy, and transport in one place. Each centre is designed as an all-day experience — shop, dine, watch a movie, bowl or skate, test-drive a car, and charge your EV while you’re here.

  • • Scale: 150–200,000 m² | 350–400 stores
  • • Energy: ~20 MW rooftop solar | ~1,000 EV chargers
  • • Transport: bus interchanges + rail where viable
  • • Build: fast large-span system (Formsteel)

Why it exists

Give New Zealanders reasons to stay, spend, and connect — by making regions exciting again and pairing global choice with local life.

  • • Counter online & overseas spend
  • • Keep Kiwis local; attract returners
  • • Support NZ start-ups alongside global brands
  • • Safe, all-weather family day out (free shows & events)
  • • Get kids off screens with real experiences
  • • Regions compete with Auckland on choice & buzz
  • • One-stop design reduces car use; PT works at this scale
  • • Inclusive jobs at scale; GDP uplift

Point of difference

Not nine isolated malls — a single national platform: common design, leasing, energy, data, and delivery. Big enough to attract anchors like IKEA, Costco, IMAX, Sports Direct. Built to feel like a day out, not an errand.

Why the scale matters

Destination effect

Scale turns retail into an attraction. Families stay all day; dwell time drives viability.

Anchor footprint

Global brands enter when they can open at scale with logistics and presence from day one.

Regional gravity

Creates a place people travel to — keeping spend and pride local.

Pays for the magic

IMAX, rinks, simulators, large-format stores only pencil at this catchment.

Flagship: Hamilton

Drawings complete. Flagship site ready for partner discussions. Designed as an all-day destination with anchor tenants, entertainment spine, EV supercharging, and a public‑transport interchange.

  • • Catchment ≈ strong Waikato growth corridor
  • • Option structure in place (details under NDA)
  • • Target anchors: IKEA | Costco | New supermarket entrant | IMAX
  • • Energy JV pathway with major power generator/retailer
Hamilton masterplan placeholder image

Delivery model

Fast-Track Approval (FTA) pathway with land under option to de-risk acquisition. Standardised large-span build system (Formsteel) for speed and cost control.

Energy & EV

~20 MW rooftop solar enabling ~1,000 concurrent EV charges. JV discussions with major power generator/retailer Energy for rollout and operations.

Entertainment spine

IMAX + multiplex, 36-lane bowling, ice / roller rinks in selected cities, flight & motorsport simulators.
